About Me


Hey, I'm Adrian.

I’m currently a senior studying mechanical engineering and design at Northwestern University. Here, I’ve been able to follow my interests and work hard to become a versatile, detail-oriented engineer, encountering new challenges along the way.

I learned that I enjoy solving problems methodically, working at the intersection of design and engineering, where I get to research problem backgrounds, create effective solutions, and work with other talented people.

I’m from Northlake, IL, but my family is originally from Durango, Mexico. When I’m not focused on coursework and projects, I enjoy keeping up with most of Chicago’s professional sports, working out, and playing sports with friends, as I’m a pretty competitive person.
